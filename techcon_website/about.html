Our History

Since its first edition in 2012, TechCon has grown from a local meetup into a global conference where developers, designers, and researchers share ideas that shape the future of technology.

What began as a small gathering is now a multi-track event with hands-on workshops, product showcases, and community networking.

Our Mission

TechCon exists to democratize knowledge, celebrate open collaboration, and empower people everywhere to build accessible, ethical, and sustainable technologies.
